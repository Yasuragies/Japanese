Introduction to japanese verb conjugation.
In Japanese verbs are devided into 2 category: Ichidan and Godan. Ichidan verb
In this page we're going to learn how to conjugate both ichidan and godan verb into past form.
verb can only fall into 1 category, either they're ichidan or godan.

Ichidan verb conjugation.
Ichidan verb include:
食べる = たべる = to eat
見る = みる = to see/watch
寝る = ねる = to sleep

to turn ichidan verb into past form just remove the る in the end of the verb and add た　

食べる -> 食べた
eat -> ate

見る ->　見た
see -> saw

寝る -> 寝た
sleep -> slept

Example

ふみこ は 昨日、ラーメンを食べた
Fumiko ate ramen yesterday

昨日 は あの 映画 を 見た (I) watch that movie yesterday.

今日 は もう寝た
(I) already slept today.
Godan verb conjugation.
Godan verbs include:
会う = あう = to meet
待つ = まつ = to wait
帰る = かえる = to return home
読む = よむ = to read
遊ぶ = あそぶ = to play
死ぬ = しぬ = to die
聞く = きく = to listen
話す = はなす = to speak


All the verb that ended other than る is godan.

Careful. 帰る and 見る both ended with る but 帰る is godan and 見る is ichidan.
you must remember which one is godan and which one is ichidan.

Godan verbs are devided into 4 categories.

Verb that ended with う・つ・る
会う・待つ・帰る

Verb that ended with む・ぶ・ぬ
読む・遊ぶ・死ぬ

Verb that ended with く
聞く

Verb that ended with す
話す

conjugate う・つ・る　to past form.
to conjugate these category into past form, remove the　う・つ・る part and add + った

会う -> 会った
to meet.

待つ -> 待った
wait -> waited

帰る -> 帰った
return home -> returned home

conjugate む・ぶ・ぬ　to　to past form.
to conjugate these category into past form, remove the　む・ぶ・ぬ part and add + んだ

読む -> 読んだ
read -> has read

遊ぶ -> 遊んだ
play -> played

死ぬ -> 死んだ
die -> died

conjugate く　into　to past form.
to conjugate く to past form remove く and add ＋ いた

聞く　-> 聞いた
hear -> heard

conjugate く　into　to past form.
to conjugate く to past form remove く and add ＋ いた

聞く　-> 聞いた
hear -> heard

conjugate す　into　to past form.
to conjugate す to past form remove す and add ＋ した

話した　-> 話した
speak -> spoke

Example

たなか は 昨日、十時に帰った
Tanaka returned home at 10 yesterday

先週 は 駅で ふみこ を 待った
(I) waited for Fumiko last week at the sation.

こばやし は 昨日、 公園 で 遊んだ
Kobayashi played at the park yesterday.
ふみこ は　昨晩、音楽 を 聞いた Fumiko listened to a music last night.
